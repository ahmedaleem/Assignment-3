Favorite Tourist Spots

Hunza Valley

Hunza Valley, located in the Gilgit-Baltistan region, is renowned for its stunning landscapes, snow-capped peaks, and serene environment. The valley is surrounded by some of the world's highest mountains, including Rakaposhi and Ultar Sar.

Skardu

Skardu, also in Gilgit-Baltistan, is a gateway to some of the world's highest peaks, including K2. The region is famous for its beautiful lakes such as Shangrila Lake, Sheosar Lake, and the tranquil Upper Kachura Lake.

Fairy Meadows

Fairy Meadows offers breathtaking views of Nanga Parbat, the world's ninth-highest mountain. It is a popular trekking destination and provides a picturesque camping experience amid lush green meadows and alpine forests.
